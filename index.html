Hello, I’m Nishant Gode, a data-first strategist with 5+ years of experience driving $75M+ in business impact across analytics, marketing operations, and product strategy. I specialize in transforming raw data into insights that scale growth, retention, and decision clarity.

I hold a Master’s in Business Analytics (GPA 3.8) from Arizona State University, a PGDM in Marketing, and a Bachelor’s in Hospitality Management. My technical toolkit includes SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, applied across initiatives like Teradyne’s inventory optimization ($36M savings), Trader Joe’s GTM segmentation (1M+ transactions, $15M merchandising lift), and sentiment modeling of 10K+ Yelp reviews using BERTopic and NER.
